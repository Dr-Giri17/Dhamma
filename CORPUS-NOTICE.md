## SuttaCentral Bilara editions

Only translations verified against Bilara's published branch and CC0 statement
are imported. Each edition retains its own pinned source revision, source path,
translator, and CC0 metadata.

## Theravada.ru editions

Imported Russian HTML editions retain a direct source-page URL, the named
translator when present (or an explicit unknown value), and the recorded
translation basis. Theravada.ru requests a direct link to its site when
materials are copied. Pages with conflicting notices and third-party PDF-only
resources are excluded.

Downstream users must inspect the per-edition corpus manifest and must not treat
the complete corpus as MIT-licensed.
