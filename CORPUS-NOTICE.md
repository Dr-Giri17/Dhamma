## SuttaCentral Bilara editions

Only translations verified against Bilara's published branch and CC0 statement
are imported. Each edition retains its own pinned source revision, source path,
translator, and CC0 metadata.

Milindapañha and Peṭakopadesa occur in that VRI navigation but are labelled
tradition-dependent. This repository does not claim that every Theravāda
edition classifies them identically.

## Russian editions

Only the five previously verified Bilara seed editions are retained: MN 10,
MN 118, DN 31, SN 56.11, and Snp 1.8. The bulk Theravada.ru import is excluded.
A request to provide a direct link is not treated as a standard license for
mass redistribution, and mutable live pages are not immutable provenance.
Reintroduction requires a separate per-edition rights and provenance review.

Downstream users must inspect the per-edition corpus manifest and must not treat
the bundled corpus as MIT-licensed.
